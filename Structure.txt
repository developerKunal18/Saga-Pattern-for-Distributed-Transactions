🧠 Why This Is Important

Imagine placing an online order:
Order Service
      │
      ▼
Payment Service
      │
      ▼
Inventory Service
      │
      ▼
Shipping Service

What happens if Inventory Service fails after Payment succeeds?

Without Saga:
✔ Payment Completed

❌ Inventory Failed

Money deducted 😢

With Saga:
Create Order ✔
      │
Charge Payment ✔
      │
Reserve Inventory ❌
      │
Compensating Action
      ▼
Refund Payment ✔
Cancel Order ✔

Benefits:
	•	✅ Data consistency
	•	✅ Automatic rollback
	•	✅ No distributed database transactions
	•	✅ Better scalability
